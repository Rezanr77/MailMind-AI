FAQ

Questions we get
all the time

Is my email data safe and private?
Absolutely. MailMind AI processes your emails in real-time but never stores them on our servers. All data is encrypted end-to-end using AES-256. We are SOC 2 Type II certified and fully GDPR compliant. Your emails are yours — always.
Does it work with Gmail and Outlook?
Yes — MailMind AI supports Gmail (personal and Google Workspace) and Microsoft Outlook (personal and Microsoft 365). Connection takes under 60 seconds via secure OAuth. Support for Apple Mail and custom IMAP is coming in Q3 2026.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, cancel anytime with no penalties or hidden fees. Your access continues until the end of your billing period. We also offer a 14-day free trial on the Pro plan — no credit card required to start.
How does the AI learn my writing style?
During setup, you can choose a tone preset or paste a few sample emails you've written. The AI analyzes your vocabulary, sentence structure, and formality level. The longer you use it, the more accurately it matches your voice — and you can retrain it at any time from settings.
Which languages does MailMind AI support?
We support 32 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Mandarin, Arabic, Hindi, and more. The AI auto-detects the sender's language and can reply in the same language or translate to your preferred one.
Is there a limit on the free Starter plan?
The Starter plan includes 50 AI-generated replies per month, unlimited email summaries, and 3 tone presets — completely free forever. No credit card, no expiry. When you're ready for unlimited replies and advanced features, upgrading to Pro takes one click.
Does MailMind AI send emails automatically?
Never without your approval. MailMind AI drafts replies for you to review — you always have the final say before anything is sent. The auto-draft feature on Business plans queues responses for your review; it doesn't send autonomously.
